About

Dr. Manikanta holds a PhD in Economics and is currently working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at St. Joseph’s College for Women (Autonomous), affiliated to Andhra University.

His academic interests include development economics, rural development, political economy, public finance, and governance. His research focuses on the interaction between institutions, policy, and development outcomes in the Indian context.

He is also keen on disseminating academic research through digital platforms to make economic ideas accessible to students, researchers, and policymakers.
